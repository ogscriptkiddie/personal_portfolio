About Me

I am a cybersecurity-focused IT professional with a strong foundation in troubleshooting, network security, and IT service management.
With hands-on experience at Aviso Wealth and the University of the Fraser Valley, I have developed expertise in Active Directory management, endpoint security, and system administration.
In my roles as a Support Analyst and Service Desk Analyst, I have successfully resolved IT incidents with a 95%+ SLA compliance rate, contributed to knowledge base development, and optimized IT workflows to enhance efficiency.
I am skilled in cybersecurity frameworks, SIEM management, and risk assessments, ensuring proactive threat mitigation.
Holding certifications in CompTIA Security+, Google Cybersecurity, and UBC’s Cybersecurity Strategy & Risk Management, I am committed to leveraging my technical expertise to strengthen organizational security and improve IT operations.
